Day Care Provider

Rosy Dhiman

Rosy is the day care provider at Cuddle Cub Day Care. She is calm, patient, and enjoys helping children feel safe and confident. Before starting day care, she spent more than six years teaching and mentoring students as an Assistant Professor in Computer Science.

In Ontario, Rosy has also worked as an Educational Assistant with the Bluewater District School Board. That experience helped her support children in a school setting and better understand how important patience, routine, and gentle encouragement are for young learners.
