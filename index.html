Meet the Owner

Rosy Dhiman

Rosy brings a calm, patient, and education-focused approach to Cuddle Cub Day Care. Her background includes more than six years as an Assistant Professor in Computer Science, where she supported students through structured learning, mentoring, classroom routines, and project-based activities.

In Ontario, Rosy has also worked as an Educational Assistant with the Bluewater District School Board, adding hands-on experience supporting children in a Canadian school environment. Her daycare approach is rooted in safety, kindness, communication, and helping each child feel comfortable, confident, and cared for.
